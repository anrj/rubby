**Risk 2: Scope Creep**

**Description:** The Visionary's role is to think big, but this could lead to adding too many features to the MVP, making it impossible to complete within the semester.

**Likelihood:** Medium

**Impact:** High

**Mitigation Plan:**
-   Strictly adhere to the "Major Decisions" process (unanimous agreement) for adding any new feature to the MVP.
-   Clearly label features as "MVP" vs. "Post-Semester" in the backlog.
-   Nikoloz (Backend Lead) is responsible for raising concerns about the technical effort required for new ideas.

**Early Warning Signs:**
-   The prioritized backlog for the MVP grows week after week.
-   Team members feel overwhelmed by the number of "P1" tasks.

**Escalation Path:**
-   If the team cannot agree on the scope, consult the team contract's conflict resolution steps.
